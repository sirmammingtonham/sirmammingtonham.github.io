yeeb bot

Started as a meme to confuse my friends on discord. Ended as a meme to confuse everyone on discord. Utilizes multiple python libraries to implement commands with some pretty funny and (sometimes) useful functionality. My favorites are the "shitty hearthstone" command group, which starts a game of HearthStone in the discord text channel, with each player typing in action indexes to play cards and attack. Also has multiple Music commands, with the ability to search and queue songs from youtube, then play them directly into the voice channel.

The bot also has multiple event listeners, which sends a meme after certain words are typed into the chat (when you least expect it).

Some libraries used include:

  • Discord.py
  • asyncio
  • BeautifulSoup4
  • MediaWikiAPI
  • Fireplace
  • NumPy
  • dateutil
  • youtube-dl
  • apex_legends
